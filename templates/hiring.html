Marketing Director

A marketing director has many roles. You may start your day being creative and developing a new marketing plan, transition to creating a budget with the accounting team, negotiate costs with suppliers in the afternoon, and finish your day with a business development team meeting. Each role requires a slightly different skill set in order to work with designers, lawyers, accountants, and business managers.

  • Designing and implementing the marketing plan
  • Planning and administering the marketing budget
  • Take in charge of social media channel
  • Negotiate with media agencies
  • Contact with music education institutions
  • An avid user of Wunderlist and other productivity apps who knows just how much fun it is to check off a to-do
  • 4+ years of experience in managing SEO initiatives, preferably for a company that indexes user generated content
  • Detailed understanding of the latest ASO techniques, and passionate following of ongoing changes to ASO
  • Strong communication/collaboration skills with a proven ability to work with both technical and business teams
  • An analytical mind who is able to evaluate user activity across all digital mediums
