What is Guitar Toolkit


Guitar Toolkit is a collection of inspiring tools for aspiring guitar players. It helps your daily practice in following ways:

  1. 1. A pro-level tuner keeps your guitar always in tune.
  2. 2. A powerful metronome holds your rhythm.
  3. 3. 108 musical scales.
  4. 4. Interactive chord diagrams with sound sample.
  5. 5. Backward chord search tells you the name of chord.
  6. 6. Learn chord combinations and progressions.

With EUM Guitar Toolkit, your favourite guitar tools are always in your pocket.

Extremely Accurate Tuner

Guitar Toolkit features a chromatic tuner which helps you get your guitar in tune in mere seconds. Colored tuning bars guide you to a great tuning quickly and clearly.
An extra meter makes you an expert who can go from great to perfect tuning. Audio samples of strings in tune allow you to tune by ear, great for developing your musical hearing.
Most importantly, it is highly accurate (up to ±1/1000 of a semitone, i.e. ±0.1 cent precise).

Precision Metronome

The metronome benefits from EUM's RTP (Real-Time Playback) technology. It is extremely precise (±20μs).

You can follow the beat easily, even in silent mode. With the help of the dial wheel, changing tempo is really simple.

Changing time signatures is only two taps away. Set it to the time signature you need. Up to 9 built-in metronome tones guide your rhythm and the 12 built-in strumming patterns will teach you how to play along easily.

Musical Scales

Scales are important for a guitarist, it is the spirits of different music styles and will lead you towards a roadmap of improvised solo and melody composition.

We provide 108 different scales, including the most popular ones such as Blues and Jazz as well as the exotic international styles like Japanese or Arabian.

To complete mastery of the guitar,
this is absolutely a must have!

Interactive Guitar Tabs

The chord tabs contain thousands of chords, more chords than you will probably ever need, including Major, Minor, 6, 7, Minor 7, Major 7, Major 9, sus 4, etc.

Not only can you see the tabs, you can also hear what they sound like. This is quite useful for beginners who learn chords but also for advanced users who are looking for inspiration.

You can look up a chord quickly by notes if you know its name (Gmaj7, for example). But in fact several chords are closely related to each other. Therefore beside note names, you are also able to see the most commonly related chords by the key signatures on the same page.

Chords Backward Search

What if you discover an interesting chord when you are playing spontaneously? Simply tap the frets, the App will tell you which chord it is. It's a fantastic feature for digging new chords.

Chord Progressions

Chord progressions help you combine chords to real songs. Did you know that many popular songs share same combination of chords? Once you discover the secret of chord progressions, you will be able to play thousands of songs and become a guitar master.
