Other Cool Features

  • Mixer

    Mix down your recorded instrumental tracks as you wish.

  • Display strumming pattern

    Forgotten how to strum the song? This feature reminds you of the correct rhythm.

  • Loop

    Focus on practicing tricky parts by using our looper.

  • Difficulty levels

    Learn either the original or simplified version of your favorite songs.

  • Redeem ticket

    Complete simple tasks to earn free new songs.

  • Left-handed mode

    iUke allows users to swap over the display so that left-handed people can join in the fun!

  • Multiple tunings

    Uke supports three ukulele tunings:

    • Tenor (GCEA)
    • Soprano (ADF#B)
    • Baritone (DGBE)

  • Share

    Recordings can be uploaded to YouTube and SoundCloud internally through the app.

  • Universal

    Sync iUke to both your iPhone and iPad.
