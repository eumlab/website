About Us

EUMlab is a group of software developers who are passionate about making excellent tools for musicians.

Founded in 2012, we make apps that help people learn, practice and perform music. These include Guitar Master and Bass Toolkit, the powerful insTuner, and a group of tools for the world’s Ukulele players. Our Pro Metronome was recently feature in Apple’s WWDC 2014 showcase – all together our tools have been downloaded over 4.5 million times.

Everyone who works for EUMlab is a musician themselves and we believe that music is the universal language of humankind.

We are based in beautiful Hangzhou, China and exciting Berlin, Germany.
