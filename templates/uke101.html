Learning to play ukulele
couldn't be easier

Uke101 uses 29 lessons to teach you, using lots of sound samples, pictures, videos, and visual notation.
Now it's easier than ever to learn different ukulele techniques,
from reading tabs or tuning to advanced chord progressions and scales.
See all lessons

One chord, two perspectives

Instead of just showing you chord fingering in 3rd person perspective like other apps,
Uke101 shows you how to play a chord using the first person perspective.
Just the way you would if you were holding a ukulele in your hand.

Interactive content

Everything that was boring to learn from a book is interesting in Uke101!

  • Learn from games

    This is where the real fun starts. Practice chords, different types of strumming, scales, and even the music theory you learned in the lessons.

  • Magic chord trainer

    Changing chords quickly is the major challenge for most beginners. With the magic chord trainer, you play along with chords and Uke101 tells you how you're doing.

  • Play and sing it!

    Uke101 has 59 tracks you can play along with. Record what you’ve learned and share it with your friends. You’ll be surprised at all the songs you can play on a uke!
