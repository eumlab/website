Introducing Rhythm Trainer
Tune the Metronome in Your Head!

We don't want to let you stuck on a metronome forever
Therefore we designed the Rhythm Trainer to train your brain.

For example, you can set mute one bar after playing one bar.
During the mute bar, you should keep counting beats in your brain.
When sound come back, you can check if your counting is synced.
You can also increase the difficulty by setting more mute bars.
